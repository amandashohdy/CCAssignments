Given a map pre-filled with student names as keys and grades as values, complete main() by reading in the name of 
a student, outputting their original grade, and then reading in and outputting their new grade.

Ex: If the input is:

Quincy Wraight
73.1
the output is:

Quincy Wraight's original grade: 65.4
Quincy Wraight's new grade: 73.1
